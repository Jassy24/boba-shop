Instagram Worthy Boba

Moshi Moshi Boba Cafe offers a vibrant blend of creative drinks, freshly made Taiyaki, and a whimsical ambiance that delights both locals and visitors.

From creamy milk teas to colorful fruit blends, every drink is made to be as photogenic as it is delicious. Our signature toppings and rotating seasonal specials make every visit a little different and always worth sharing.

A destination for those who appreciate unique flavors and cozy aesthetics


Located at 2418 Spring Garden St, Moshi Moshi is a bright, colorful, and thoughtfully decorated space filled with cute backdrops, ideal for casual hangouts or photos with friends. The staff are known for being friendly and attentive, enhancing the overall vibe of warmth and welcome.


Whether you're here to study, relax, or catch up over a sweet treat, Moshi Moshi creates the perfect setting. Enjoy the comforting aroma of freshly made Taiyaki, sip on a handcrafted drink, and let our cozy atmosphere turn any day into a little celebration.
